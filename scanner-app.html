Case Study

Scanner App Redesign

Redesigned the mobile scanner app to deliver faster, smarter scans with a modern UI and seamless user experience.

Role: Lead Product Manager
Year: 2022
Platform: Mobile Application
Industry: Security

Project Overview

The scanner app was designed to detect and remove threats on mobile devices. However, its outdated interface and slow scanning speed discouraged users from regular use.

The redesign focused on speed, user trust, and usability â€” delivering faster scans, clearer results, and an engaging interface that encouraged adoption.

My Role & Responsibilities

  • Product Strategy: Defined product vision and aligned with company mobile-first strategy.
  • User Research: Collected insights on scan frequency, trust signals, and user expectations.
  • UX Redesign: Collaborated with designers to simplify flows and add visual clarity.
  • Engineering Collaboration: Oversaw technical implementation and ensured performance gains.
  • KPIs: Monitored scan completion rates, app retention, and Net Promoter Score (NPS).

Key Challenges

  • Lengthy scan times discouraged adoption.
  • Unclear scan results reduced user confidence.
  • Outdated design failed to meet user expectations for modern apps.

Solutions & Impact

Optimized the scan engine for 2x faster results, introduced color-coded trust signals, and added contextual explanations for detected threats. Delivered a fresh, modern UI aligned with mobile design standards.

The redesign doubled daily active scans, improved retention by 35%, and boosted NPS by 25 points.

Learnings & Future Directions

This project highlighted the importance of performance in mobile UX. Future iterations could explore predictive threat detection and integration with wearable devices for real-time alerts.
