From PhD Research to Product Leadership

My journey began with a PhD in Human-Computer Interaction, where I developed expertise in Latent Semantic Analysis (LSA), cognitive user modeling, and AI-powered usability simulation. This academic foundation provided me with a unique perspective on how users interact with technology at a fundamental level.

Over the past decade, I've translated this deep technical knowledge into business success, leading cross-functional teams to deliver scalable SaaS solutions that drive measurable growth. My approach combines rigorous research methodologies with agile product development, ensuring that every decision is backed by data and user insights.

Today, I specialize in integrating NLP, machine learning, and automation into high-impact product ecosystems, helping organizations achieve digital transformation while maintaining a relentless focus on user experience and business outcomes.
