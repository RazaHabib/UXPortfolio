About Me

A passionate UX designer and product manager with over 8 years of experience transforming complex challenges into intuitive digital solutions.

My Journey

As a PhD-qualified UX Designer and Product Manager, I bring a unique blend of academic rigor and practical experience to every project. My journey began with a deep fascination for understanding how people interact with technology, which led me to pursue advanced studies in user experience design and cognitive modeling.

Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of working with diverse teams across various industries, from cybersecurity to e-commerce, always with a focus on creating meaningful user experiences. My approach combines data-driven insights with human-centered design principles, ensuring that every solution not only meets business objectives but also delights users.

I'm particularly passionate about B2B applications and complex systems where thoughtful design can dramatically improve productivity and user satisfaction. My experience leading cross-functional teams has taught me the importance of collaboration, clear communication, and maintaining a user-first mindset throughout the product development process.
