Case Study

Customer Upgrade Center Redesign

Redesigned the customer upgrade center to streamline the upgrade process and improve conversion rates.

Role: Product Manager
Year: 2022 (G DATA CyberDefence AG)
Platform: Web Application
Industry: E-commerce/B2C

Project Overview

The existing customer upgrade center had a complex, multi-step process that was causing significant drop-offs during the upgrade journey. Customers were abandoning their upgrade attempts due to unclear pricing, confusing feature comparisons, and a lengthy checkout process.

This project aimed to redesign the entire upgrade experience to reduce friction, improve clarity around product benefits, and ultimately increase conversion rates while maintaining customer satisfaction.

My Role & Responsibilities

  • **Product Leadership:** Led the product vision and strategy for the customer upgrade center redesign, focusing on optimizing the user journey and conversion funnels.
  • **UX Research & Design:** Conducted conversion funnel analysis and customer feedback reviews. Designed simplified information architecture and value-focused feature presentations.
  • **A/B Testing & Optimization:** Implemented and analyzed A/B tests on key elements like pricing display and checkout flow to drive data-informed design decisions.
  • **Cross-Functional Collaboration:** Collaborated with engineering, marketing, and sales teams to ensure seamless integration and successful launch of the redesigned upgrade center.
  • **Impact Measurement:** Tracked key metrics such as conversion rates, customer support inquiries, and customer lifetime value to demonstrate business impact.

Key Challenges

  • High drop-off rates during the multi-step upgrade process.
  • Unclear pricing and confusing feature comparisons.
  • Lengthy and cumbersome checkout experience.
  • Balancing business goals of increased conversion with user needs for clarity and ease of use.

Solutions & Impact

Implemented a simplified information architecture and value-focused design for feature presentations. Created a clear, visual plan comparison and transparent pricing display. Redesigned the checkout process for progressive information collection and clear progress indicators. Added personalized recommendations and interactive feature demos.

The redesign resulted in a **25% increase in upgrade conversion rates**, a **45% reduction in friction points**, and a **30% faster upgrade process**. Customer support inquiries related to upgrades decreased by 40%.
